1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you. This includes personal identification information such as your name, telephone number, email address, and home address when you book an appointment online or contact our clinic.

Additionally, we collect clinical and medical history information necessary for providing dental treatments safely. This include records of previous dental work, active health conditions, allergies, list of medications, digital X-rays, and treatment charts generated during your visits to Sunny Dental Surgery.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various clinical and administrative purposes, including scheduling and managing dental appointments, communicating updates or confirmation via WhatsApp/SMS, and generating accurate treatment charts. Medical records are used solely by our clinical team to plan and deliver dental procedures safely.

Administrative staff utilize your contact details for invoicing, payment processing, sending aftercare instructions, and following up on healing progress. We do not use your medical data for any commercial marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We respect your medical privacy. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable or medical information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

We may share your records with external medical laboratories when manufacturing custom dental crowns, veneers, aligners or braces. In such cases, only the minimum required dental models and initials are shared under strict data protection protocols.

4. Data Security

The security of your medical and personal data is important to us. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. Digital patient records are stored on secure, encrypted servers with restricted access limited to authorised clinical personnel only.

Paper records, if any, are kept in locked clinical storage. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security, as no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Under clinical standards and local regulations, you have the right to request access to your dental records, treatment history, and digital X-rays. You can request copies of these records for transfer to other clinics or specialists if required.

You also have the right to correct any inaccurate contact details in our database. To make a request, contact our clinic front desk with valid proof of identity.

6. Cookies

Our website utilizes cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

We use cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

7. Children's Privacy

We provide paediatric dentistry services, which involves collecting information about minors. We do not collect any personal information from children under the age of 18 without explicit parental or legal guardian consent.

All child patient records are managed through the parents' or guardians' primary accounts, ensuring full visibility and control over their child's medical records.
