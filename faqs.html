A knocked-out (avulsed) tooth is a dental emergency that requires immediate action. Time is critical — the sooner the tooth is re-implanted, the better the chance of saving it. Please follow these steps:

  • Pick up the tooth by the crown (the white part), not the root
  • If dirty, rinse gently with milk or saline — do not scrub or use tap water
  • Place the tooth back in its socket immediately if possible, or store it in milk or between your cheek and gum
  • Come to Sunny Dental Surgery immediately — ideally within 30 minutes

Dental implants are designed to be a permanent, lifelong solution for missing teeth. The titanium implant fixture that integrates with your jawbone can last a lifetime with proper care. The crown placed on top of the implant typically lasts 15–25 years before it may need replacement due to normal wear.

To maximise the longevity of your implant, we recommend regular dental check-ups (every 6 months), diligent daily brushing and flossing, and avoiding habits such as smoking or biting very hard foods. Our team will provide comprehensive aftercare guidance following every implant procedure.

For most adults, a dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months is the standard recommendation. This allows our team to detect early signs of tooth decay, gum disease, or other oral health issues before they become costly or painful problems.

However, some patients may benefit from more frequent visits — for example, those with a history of gum disease, active orthodontic treatment, or a high cavity risk. Your dentist will personalise a recall schedule based on your individual oral health profile during your first visit.

  • General adults: every 6 months
  • Children: every 6 months (more frequently if high cavity risk)
  • Orthodontic patients: as directed by your orthodontist
  • High-risk patients: every 3–4 months
